My Favorite Artists and Albums

End Points, responses in Json

Get list of favorite artists with GET method:

/getListFavArtistsJson

Search an Artist and obtain the favorite albums with GET method:

/searchFavArtistJson/"artist here"

Get list favorite albums of an artists with GET method:

/getListFavAlbumsJson/"artist here"

Add new favorite artist with POST method:

/insertArtistJSon/"artist here"

Add new favorite album from an artist with POST method:

/insertArtistAlbumJson/"artist here"/"album here"

Delete a favorite artist record (with its albums) with DELETE method:

/removeFavArtist/"artist here"

Get favorite artist' album art with GET method (Powered by LastFM):

/getAlbumArtJson/"artist name here"/"album here"

Get tracks of one of my favorite artist' albums with GET method (Powered by LastFM):

/getAlbumTracksJson/"artist name here"/"album here"
